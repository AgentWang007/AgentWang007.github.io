My name is Jason Wang and I hail from the state of NJ. Prior to working toward a programmer, I sought other paths in life such as medicine and law. However as I grew older, I also realized that I wasn't losing my sense of self undergoing these careers. I've alway enjoyed being engaged in technology and I hope to solidify my understanding in the years ahead.

I'm NOT a closet nerd. I love comic books, action movies, and video games unabashedly. When people state that you grow out of these hobbies, I feel it is disingenous to state that. If anything, you lose time to play with these hobbies as you get older. However, I remain passionate about my hobbies even with less time in my life. I also love dogs since they're absolutely the best non-human friends you could ask for.
